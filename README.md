# Swiftrail Logistics Company

## About Us

We are a logistics company specializing in managing massive or heavy goods and shipments using rail networks as a primary element, combined with land transport (trucks) to provide a free and cost-effective door-to-door transport service.

---

## ⚠️ The Problem

Shipments/goods delays

## 🤖 Why an Agent is Needed

In rail freight, a delay to one train quickly creates a domino effect of blocked tracks, missed schedules, and costly network logjams.

Traditional tracking tools only warn you when a train is late, but an autonomous AI agent actively fixes the problem in seconds. It predicts bottlenecks hours in advance, prioritizes high-value cargo, and automatically updates schedules or redirects trains.

By replacing slow human coordination with instant execution, it keeps swift rail networks running smoothly and stops small delays from escalating into major disasters.
